My personal opinion

I used to smoke tobacco for about 5 years before quitting in the age of 20. It took me several unsuccessfull attempts before I came across brilliant book “Easy way” by Allen Carr. Back then their was a huge polarization in Ukraine as per situation with drugs. With heavy tobacco and alcohol abuse on one side and huge problems caused by heroin eddiction on the other one.

I started writing this article simply because I’ve accumulated a fair amount of information about drugs over drugs abuse several years. It would be fair to say, that this topic was always interesting to me due to it’s semi-legal nature.

However, the first shift in my perception was initiated by my friends, whos was deeply involved into martial arts. Thanks to him, I was able to finally hover over primitive view of some drugs as simply way of party hard and discover a spiritial component, that is largely ignored by most people.

I was also influenced to a large extent by Peter Watts’s view on drugs, that he has brilliantly outlined in his series Rifters. This is an outstanding example of a modern “hardcore” science fiction. Though I should say, part of the reason it had such a huge influence on my perception of this topic was, that I stumbled across his book at the right period of my life. Also because Watts was able to summerise with incredible sensitivity a bunch of ideas, that were kind of floating around for some time already.

Last but not the least, many of Joe Rogan’s podcasts and his guests (Paul Stemets etc) provided me with interesting ideas regarding cannabis, DMT, cillosyben etc.
